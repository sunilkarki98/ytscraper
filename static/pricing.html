Frequently Asked Questions

What emails do you extract?
We extract publicly available email addresses from YouTube channel "About" pages and descriptions. These are emails that creators have voluntarily published for business inquiries. We also extract social links (Instagram, TikTok, X, LinkedIn, Facebook).
Do unused emails roll over?
Monthly plans reset each billing cycle. Unused emails do not roll over. We recommend choosing a plan that matches your regular usage.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.
Is this legal?
We only access publicly available information that creators have voluntarily published on their YouTube channel pages. By using our service, you agree to our Terms of Service, which require lawful use of all extracted data in compliance with CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and other applicable regulations.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and selected digital wallets via Stripe. Enterprise customers can request invoice-based billing.
How fast is the scraping?
Speed varies by niche and proxy availability. On average, you can expect 30–60 emails per minute. Results are streamed in real-time so you can see progress instantly.
