Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026  |  Effective immediately

This Privacy Policy describes how LAGIC ("Company", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use YouTube Email Scraper Pro ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

Data Type What Why
Account Data Email address, name, hashed password Authentication and account management
Usage Data Jobs run, emails scraped, credit usage Service delivery and billing
Technical Data IP address, browser type, access timestamps Security monitoring and abuse prevention
Payment Data Processed by Stripe — we never store card numbers Credit purchases

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your browsing activity outside of the Service.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service delivery: Processing your scraping jobs and delivering results.
  • Account management: Authentication, credit tracking, and communication.
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve the Service (aggregated, non-personal).

4. Data Scraped by the Service

The Service extracts publicly available information from YouTube channel pages, including email addresses, social media links, channel names, and subscriber counts. This data:

  • Is sourced exclusively from publicly visible YouTube channel descriptions.
  • Is stored in your account for the retention period described below.
  • Is your responsibility to use in compliance with applicable laws.
  • Is not shared between user accounts.

5. Data Retention

  • Free users: Scraping results are retained for 7 days, then permanently deleted.
  • Paid users: Extended retention as specified in your plan.
  • Account data: Retained until you request deletion or 12 months after last login.
  • Technical logs: Retained for 30 days for security purposes.

6. Data Security

  • Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2-SHA256 with random salt (never stored in plain text).
  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Database access is restricted and authenticated.
  • JWT tokens expire after 72 hours.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format (CSV/JSON).
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@lagic.com.
